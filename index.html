What is Sportiva?

Sportiva is a SportsTech company operating in the digital sports services and marketplace domain. We offer a comprehensive platform that connects players and sports facility owners across Egypt, simplifying the process of booking, managing, and engaging with the sports community.

Instant Booking

Find and reserve sports fields with real-time slot visibility.

Player Community

Connect with others to find games, join teams, or create tournaments.

Field Management

A dashboard for owners to list availability, handle bookings, and run promotions.

Sports Marketplace

An in-app shop for gear, offering a targeted sales channel for local vendors.

How It Works

Your game is just a few clicks away.

1. Find Your Pitch

Use our advanced filters to find the perfect field based on location, price, and rating.

2. Book Instantly

Select your desired time slot and confirm your booking with secure online payment.

3. Just Play!

Show up at the field at your booked time. We handle the rest. It's that simple.
