Our Story

Sportiva was born from a simple, yet common frustration: the hassle of booking a football pitch. For over a decade, our founder, Marwan Magdy, experienced firsthand the endless phone calls, uncertain availability, and coordination headaches that came with organizing a game. He knew there had to be a better way.

Fueled by a passion for both sports and technology, the idea for Sportiva took shape. It wasn't just about booking; it was about creating a seamless experience for everyone. An all-in-one platform where players could instantly find and book fields, connect with teammates, and even organize their own tournaments, while empowering facility owners with the digital tools to grow their business. Today, Sportiva is poised to lead Egypt's digital sports transformation.

Our Mission

To build a trusted and widely-used platform for booking sports facilities across Egypt, helping field owners increase their income by filling more slots, and fostering a strong sports community where players can easily find games and teammates.
